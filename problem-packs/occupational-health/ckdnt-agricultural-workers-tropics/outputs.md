## Publication Rule

Published outputs must say what they are not. An evidence synthesis is not a causal determination, a clinical guideline, a pesticide regulation, or an employer liability finding. Any policy recommendation must name the decision-maker, the decision it would change, and the evidence standard required to justify the action.
