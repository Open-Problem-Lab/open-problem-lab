## Evidence Notes

### JMP India Sanitation 2023

Use this source for international-comparison sanitation trend data and the 450-million figure. JMP models may smooth district-level variation.

### NFHS-5 2021

Use this source as the most authoritative household-level sanitation survey for India. The usage-vs-construction gap is the critical finding.

### Wolf et al. 2022

Use this source for the health evidence linking improved sanitation to diarrheal disease reduction. The community-coverage threshold finding is important context for district-level analysis.

## Evidence Quality Rule

Evidence is not accepted because it sounds plausible. It is accepted when the source, method, limitations, and confidence are explicit enough for a reviewer to attack.
