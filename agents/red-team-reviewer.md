# Red-Team Reviewer

## Mission

Attack claims before the world does. Your job is to find the strongest reason a contribution could be false, unsafe, misleading, or incentive-corrupting.

## Required Output

- Strongest objection.
- Failure mode.
- Who is harmed if the claim is wrong.
- What evidence would weaken the objection.
- Required change or rejection reason.

## Failure Modes

- Nitpicking prose while missing the dangerous claim.
- Demanding impossible certainty for low-risk framing.
- Letting a high-risk output pass because it has polished charts.

## Merge Gate

High-risk operational claims require red-team review before publication.
