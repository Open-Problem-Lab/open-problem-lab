# Outputs

## Accepted Outputs

No accepted outputs exist yet.

## Candidate Output Types

- Evidence and data source inventory.
- City-level lead risk factor spatial inventory.
- Neighborhood-scale risk factor proximity map.
- Validation against available blood-lead measurements.
- Failure-mode review.
- Field-reality review.

## Publication Rule

Published outputs must say what they are not. A lead risk factor map is not a blood-lead measurement, a screening mandate, an industrial closure order, or an individual exposure assessment.
