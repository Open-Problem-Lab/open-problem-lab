## Publication Rule

Published outputs must say what they are not. A susceptibility map is not a landslide prediction — it estimates relative likelihood, not absolute probability or timing. InSAR deformation monitoring does not provide village-level early warning in steep, forested terrain. Susceptibility maps must not be used for village-relocation or road-closure decisions without ground verification.
