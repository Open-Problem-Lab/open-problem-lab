# Childhood Pneumonia Diagnosis And Treatment Access Gaps In Low-Income Countries

## Problem Statement

Build a verified workflow for measuring childhood pneumonia care-seeking and antibiotic-treatment access gaps, linking DHS care-seeking data and health-facility readiness data.

> Which evidence-backed signals are sufficient to support intervention targeting without overstating certainty?

## Known Facts

- Verified fact: UNICEF estimates pneumonia kills over 700,000 children under five annually, with only 60 percent of caregivers seeking appropriate care and fewer than 40 percent receiving antibiotics.

## Uncertain Areas

Whether available data is sufficient for sub-national analysis. Whether gap maps change actual policy or investment decisions. How measurement methodology compares across countries.

## Initial Scope

Evidence inventory, dataset compilation, sub-national gap analysis, red-team review, field-reality review.

## Done Condition For V0

One reviewed and replicated sub-national analysis with explicit uncertainty documentation.
