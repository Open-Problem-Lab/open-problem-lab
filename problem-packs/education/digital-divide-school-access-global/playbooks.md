# Playbooks

## Analyst Playbook

Start with `problem.json`. Add evidence before claims. Mark operational inferences reviewed/replicated/blocked.

## Reviewer Playbook

Ask: What connectivity gap claim? What mapping data? What classification method? What would misclassify a school as connected? Who could use a connectivity gap map to justify technology procurement without teacher training?

## Field-Reality Playbook

Ask whether an education ministry ICT director would change infrastructure deployment based on this map. Connectivity without electricity, devices, or teacher digital literacy does not close the divide.
