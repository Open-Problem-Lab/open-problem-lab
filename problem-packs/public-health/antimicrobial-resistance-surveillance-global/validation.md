| --------------- | ----------------------- |

## Baseline Requirements

GLASS compilation must document: enrollment status, reporting completeness, sub-national detail, quality metrics.

## Model Requirements

No surveillance gap validated without: lab-capacity linkage, burden-estimate sensitivity, replication.
