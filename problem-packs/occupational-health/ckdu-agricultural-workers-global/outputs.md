# Outputs

## Accepted Outputs

No accepted outputs exist yet.

This is deliberate. The pack is scoped, not solved.

## Candidate Output Types

- Evidence inventory.
- Prevalence compilation table.
- Causal evidence synthesis ranking heat-stress, agrochemical, and infectious-disease hypotheses.
- Intervention evidence review.
- Failure-mode review.
- Field-reality review.

## Publication Rule

Published outputs must say what they are not. A causal evidence synthesis is not a clinical diagnosis tool, a pesticide liability determination, or an occupational safety enforcement order. It does not assign blame to workers or employers for CKD cases. A hypothesis ranking is not proof that the lower-ranked hypotheses are false; it is an assessment of relative evidence strength at the time of review.
