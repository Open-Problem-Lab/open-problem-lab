# Validation

## Validation Layers

| Layer           | Gate                                                                 |
| --------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Structure       | `pnpm validate` passes schemas and problem-pack completeness checks  |
| Evidence        | Every claim has a dated evidence record                              |
| Reproducibility | Quantitative artifacts include rerunnable steps                      |
| Review          | Required reviewer roles approve or request changes                   |
| Replication     | Safety-sensitive or quantitative claims are independently reproduced |

## Baseline Requirements

Lead risk factor spatial inventories must document:

- Risk factor type and classification methodology.
- Geographic grain and coordinate reference system.
- Proximity buffer methodology and justification.
- Known missing risk factors.
- Industrial classification including formal and informal sector distinctions.

## Model Or Signal Requirements

No lead risk map may be called validated until it includes:

- Input data sources.
- Risk score methodology and weighting.
- Validation dataset including detection limits and sampling dates.
- Sensitivity, specificity, and area under curve where blood-lead data exists.
- Explicit statement of geographic areas where validation was impossible.
- Non-stigmatization guardrails including data aggregation thresholds.
- Replication status.
