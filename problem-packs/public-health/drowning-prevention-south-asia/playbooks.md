# Playbooks

Start with `problem.json`. Add evidence before claims. Mark inferences reviewed/replicated/blocked.

## Reviewer Playbook

Ask: What drowning-risk claim? What water-body and mortality data? What method? Who could misuse a risk map to justify filling ponds that are community resources?

## Field-Reality Playbook

Ask whether a public-health program manager would change creche placement based on this map. Water-body proximity is a risk factor, not a cause — supervision is the intervention.
