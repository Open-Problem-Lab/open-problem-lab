# Playbooks

## Analyst Playbook

Start with `problem.json`. Add evidence before claims. Mark operational inferences reviewed/replicated/blocked.

## Reviewer Playbook

Ask: What collection-gap claim? What waste and collection data? What method? What makes the gap wrong? Who could misuse a gap map to displace informal waste pickers or privatize collection?

## Field-Reality Playbook

Ask whether a municipal waste department would change collection routes or recycling policy based on this analysis. Informal waste pickers are major recycling contributors — mapping formal gaps without acknowledging informal systems is misleading.
