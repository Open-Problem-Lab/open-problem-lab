# Playbooks

## Analyst Playbook

Start with `problem.json`. Add evidence before claims. Mark operational inferences reviewed/replicated/blocked.

## Reviewer Playbook

Ask: What access gap claim? What facility and travel-time data? What method? What would make the gap wrong? Who could misuse it to close facilities in low-access areas?

## Field-Reality Playbook

Ask whether a district health officer would change ambulance placement or referral protocols based on this map. Travel time is not the only barrier — facility readiness, user fees, and cultural acceptability matter equally.
