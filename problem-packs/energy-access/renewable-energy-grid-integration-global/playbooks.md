# Playbooks

## Analyst Playbook

Start with `problem.json`. Add evidence before claims. Mark operational inferences reviewed/replicated/blocked.

## Reviewer Playbook

Ask: What bottleneck claim? What grid and regulatory data? What method? What makes the bottleneck classification wrong? Who could misuse it to justify utility privatization without consumer protection?

## Field-Reality Playbook

Ask whether a utility planning director or energy regulator would change grid-upgrade or procurement decisions based on this analysis. Grid bottlenecks are technical, regulatory, and political.
