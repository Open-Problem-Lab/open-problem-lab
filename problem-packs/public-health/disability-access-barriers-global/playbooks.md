# Playbooks

## Analyst Playbook

Start with `problem.json`. Add evidence before claims. Mark inferences reviewed/replicated/blocked.

## Reviewer Playbook

Ask: What access-barrier claim? What disability and facility data? What method? What makes the barrier map wrong? Who could misuse it to justify segregation of disabled populations?

## Field-Reality Playbook

Ask whether an inclusive-education or health-accessibility planner would change service design based on this analysis. Disability access barriers are physical, attitudinal, and institutional.
