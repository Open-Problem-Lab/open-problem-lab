# Validation

## Validation Layers

| Layer           | Gate                                                                             |
| --------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Structure       | `pnpm validate` passes schemas and problem-pack completeness checks              |
| Evidence        | Every causal claim has a dated evidence record with study design and limitations |
| Reproducibility | Prevalence compilations include rerunnable source extraction steps               |
| Review          | Required reviewer roles approve or request changes                               |
| Replication     | Safety-sensitive or quantitative claims are independently reproduced             |

## Prevalence Requirements

CKDu prevalence estimates must document:

- Country and sub-national region.
- Study population (occupation, age range, sex).
- CKD diagnostic criteria (eGFR threshold, albuminuria, biomarker).
- Screening method.
- Sample size and confidence interval.
- Sampling frame and recruitment method.
- Whether CKDu was separated from conventional CKD.

## Causal Evidence Requirements

No causal hypothesis may be called dominant until it includes:

- At least two independent supporting studies.
- A proposed mechanistic pathway.
- Assessment of whether the evidence can distinguish it from competing hypotheses.
- Transferability assessment across at least two endemic regions.
- Known non-use cases.
- Replication status.

## Intervention Review Requirements

No intervention recommendation may be merged without:

- Named decision-maker.
- Named allocation decision it would change.
- Efficacy evidence with study type and context.
- Cost estimate or range.
- At least one failure mode.
- Non-use cases.
