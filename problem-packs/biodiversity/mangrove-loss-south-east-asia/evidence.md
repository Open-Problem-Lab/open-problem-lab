## Evidence Notes

### Global Mangrove Watch 2024

Use GMW as the primary satellite product for mangrove-extent change detection. The 25m resolution may miss narrow fringing mangroves that provide local wave attenuation. Pre-1996 extent must be estimated from coarser sources, introducing baseline uncertainty. Aquaculture attribution relies on concurrent land-use classification — not all mangrove loss is aquaculture conversion. Document these limitations in every sub-national analysis.

### Mangrove Storm-Surge Attenuation

Use this source for the physical attenuation relationship, with the critical caveat that the 30-70 percent range depends on mangrove width, density, species, and bathymetry. Applying it to specific sites requires local mangrove-structure data rarely available in Southeast Asia. The relationship is non-linear — narrow strips under 100m provide minimal protection. Do not apply global average attenuation to specific sites without local data.

### Menendez et al. 2020 Flood Protection Valuation

Use this source for the global protection-value baseline ($65 billion annually), not for site-level planning. The figure is a global aggregate with enormous sub-national variation based on population density, infrastructure, and storm frequency. Valuation depends on damage functions assuming certain building types that may not reflect informal coastal settlements common in Southeast Asia. Values are probabilistic expected annual avoided damages, not actual damage in any specific event.

### Deforestation Rate Acceleration

Use this source for country-level trend context — Thailand and Vietnam slowing, Indonesia and Myanmar accelerating. Deforestation rates depend on satellite product version and time window. Restoration program effectiveness is difficult to assess — planted mangroves may not provide equivalent ecosystem services for decades. Driver classification requires ground verification sparse in Myanmar and eastern Indonesia.

## Evidence Quality Rule

Evidence is not accepted because it sounds plausible. It is accepted when the source, method, limitations, and confidence are explicit enough for a reviewer to attack.
