# Code Of Conduct

Open Problem Lab needs disagreement. It does not need abuse.

## Standard

Participants must:

- Argue from evidence and method.
- Mark uncertainty.
- Separate criticism of claims from attacks on people.
- Respect privacy, consent, and local context.
- Avoid spam, performative issue creation, and unverifiable claims.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may close, edit, or restrict issues and comments that undermine verification, safety, or participant dignity. Repeated abuse, spam, or unsafe behavior may lead to removal from the project.
