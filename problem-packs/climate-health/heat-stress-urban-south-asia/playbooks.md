# Playbooks

## Analyst Playbook

Start with `problem.json`. Add evidence before claims. Mark operational inferences as reviewed/replicated/blocked.

## Reviewer Playbook

Ask: What heat-risk claim? What exposure and vulnerability data? What method? What would make the map dangerously wrong? Who could misuse it to justify displacement?

## Field-Reality Playbook

Ask whether a city heat-action planner would change cooling-center placement or early-warning targeting based on this map. Heat vulnerability is not just temperature — it is access to water, shade, cooling, and healthcare.
