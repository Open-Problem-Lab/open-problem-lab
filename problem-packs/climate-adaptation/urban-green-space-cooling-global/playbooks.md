# Playbooks

## Analyst Playbook

Start with `problem.json`, `evidence.json`, and `tasks.json`. Add evidence before claims. Keep outputs reproducible. Mark operational inferences as reviewed, replicated, or blocked.

## Reviewer Playbook

Ask: What claim? What evidence? What method? What would make it wrong? Who could misuse it?

## Field-Reality Playbook

Ask whether a named decision-maker would change a named decision based on this output, and what would go wrong if the output was wrong.
