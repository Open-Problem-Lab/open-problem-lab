# Evidence Ledger

## Current Evidence Records

The machine-readable ledger is `evidence.json`.

## Evidence Notes

### Rodell et al. 2009 GRACE Study

Use this source as the landmark evidence that GRACE can detect groundwater depletion from space. It established the 109 km³ loss figure for northern India. Its spatial resolution limitation means it cannot substitute for local well monitoring.

### CGWB Groundwater Assessment 2024

Use this source for official Indian government groundwater status data. The assessment unit framework is the closest available to a national standardized depletion inventory. Individual assessment unit data should be verified against raw well records where possible.

### Fishman 2023 Governance Review

Use this source for the institutional and economic drivers context. It explains why depletion continues despite decades of awareness. Treat policy recommendations as context, not as validated interventions.

## Evidence Quality Rule

Evidence is not accepted because it sounds plausible. It is accepted when the source, method, limitations, and confidence are explicit enough for a reviewer to attack.
