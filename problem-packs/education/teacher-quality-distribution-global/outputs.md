## Candidate Output Types

- Evidence inventory with EMIS data-quality assessment and SDI country-coverage documentation.
- Sub-national teacher-qualification distribution map for at least two low-income countries.
- Deployment-equity analysis linking teacher distribution to learning outcomes with qualification-proxy limitations.
- Failure-mode review of qualification-proxy overstatement and punitive-transfer risks.
- Field-reality review with education ministry and teacher-training institution context.

## Publication Rule

Published outputs must say what they are not. A teacher-qualification distribution map is not a teaching-effectiveness measurement — qualification is a weak proxy for actual classroom performance. Deployment-equity analysis must not be used for punitive teacher transfers without addressing underlying incentive structures. Absence data must not be used to stigmatize rural teachers without documenting the systemic factors that drive absence.
