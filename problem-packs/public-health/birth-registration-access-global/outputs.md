# Outputs

## Accepted Outputs

No accepted outputs exist yet.

## Candidate Types

- Source inventory that separates survey status, certificate possession, and CRVS completeness.
- Measurement reconciliation note explaining which birth-registration metric answers which question.
- Country-selection memo identifying where sub-national work is actually feasible.
- Missed-opportunity analysis comparing birth-registration gaps against one health-system touchpoint such as facility births or immunization reach.
- Red-team review on coercive use, denominator abuse, and false comparability.
- Field-reality review with registrar, immunization, or health-information workflows.

## Publication Rule

Published outputs must say what they are not.

- A low registration estimate is not proof of caregiver refusal.
- A survey-based under-5 measure is not the same thing as live-birth registration completeness.
- A health-touchpoint gap is not proof that integration will work without workflow, staffing, and legal checks.
- A district ranking is not a license for punitive enforcement or exclusion from services.
