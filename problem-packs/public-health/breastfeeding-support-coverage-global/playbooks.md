# Playbooks

## Analyst Playbook

Start with `problem.json`, `evidence.json`, and `tasks.json`. Add evidence before claims. Keep quantitative outputs reproducible. Mark every program-relevant inference as reviewed, replicated, or blocked.

## Reviewer Playbook

Ask: What breastfeeding-support coverage claim? What DHS and BFHI data? What method produced the gap estimate? What would make the gap map dangerously wrong? Who could misuse a gap map to penalize non-certified facilities or undermine breastfeeding in communities without formal support?

## Field-Reality Playbook

Ask whether a district nutrition coordinator or maternity-ward head would change lactation-support staffing or community-outreach strategy based on this output. Breastfeeding is not just a facility issue — community norms, workplace policies, and family support determine outcomes.
