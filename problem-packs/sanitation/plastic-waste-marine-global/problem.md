# Plastic Waste Leakage Into Marine Environments From Coastal Cities

## Problem Statement

Build a verified workflow for estimating plastic waste leakage into marine environments from coastal cities, linking waste-generation data, river-plume data, and marine-debris observations.

> Which evidence-backed signals are sufficient to support decision-making without overstating certainty?

## Known Facts

- Verified fact: A 2015 Science study estimated 8 million metric tons of plastic enter the oceans annually, with 90 percent from 10 rivers in Asia and Africa, and this figure could triple by 2040.

## Uncertain Areas

Whether available data is sufficient for sub-national analysis. Whether gap maps change actual policy or investment decisions. How measurement methodology compares across countries.

## Initial Scope

Evidence inventory, dataset compilation, sub-national gap analysis, red-team review, field-reality review.

## Done Condition For V0

One reviewed and replicated sub-national analysis with explicit uncertainty documentation.
