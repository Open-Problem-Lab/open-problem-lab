# Dataset Inventory

## Candidate Sources

| Source                                | Grain                          | Current status                           | Use                                                      |
| ------------------------------------- | ------------------------------ | ---------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------- |
| National training-provider registries | Institution/district, variable | Fragmented, country-dependent            | Training-program availability mapping                    |
| Labor-force surveys (ILO standards)   | Household, periodic            | Usable, youth modules available          | Youth employment status and training participation       |
| Enterprise surveys (World Bank)       | Firm, periodic                 | Usable, limited LMIC coverage            | Employer skill demands and hiring practices              |
| ILO school-to-work transition surveys | Youth, periodic                | Usable, limited country coverage         | Training-to-employment pathway tracking                  |
| TVET management information systems   | Institution, variable          | Variable, often not public               | Training enrollment and completion data                  |
| NGO training-program documentation    | Program, variable              | Variable, often not aggregated           | Non-government training availability                     |
| Skills-mismatch assessments           | Sector/occupation, variable    | Sparse, methodology varies               | Labor-market demand-supply gap analysis                  |
| Youth labor-force modules (DHS)       | Household, periodic            | Usable, publicly available               | Youth employment and education indicators                |
| Informal-sector surveys               | Household/enterprise, variable | Sparse, measurement challenges           | Informal employment outcomes for training graduates      |

## Required Dataset Properties

- Date range and temporal resolution.
- Geographic grain and spatial resolution.
- Training-provider data completeness — explicitly document fragmentation across government, NGO, and private operators.
- Employment-outcome tracking rate — document what share of programs track graduate employment.
- Informal-sector measurement methodology.
- Access conditions and license.

## Rejection Rule

A dataset is rejected for canonical analysis if grain, date range, license, or method cannot be verified. Datasets with known limitations (e.g., fragmented provider registries, low outcome-tracking rates) may be listed as limited with explicit documentation. Rejected datasets may still be listed as context.
