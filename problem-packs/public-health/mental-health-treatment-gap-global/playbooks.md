# Playbooks

## Analyst Playbook

Start with `problem.json`. Add evidence before claims. Mark operational inferences reviewed/replicated/blocked.

## Reviewer Playbook

Ask: What treatment gap claim? What prevalence and treatment data? What method? What makes the gap wrong? Who could misuse a treatment gap map to justify involuntary treatment or to underfund prevention?

## Field-Reality Playbook

Ask whether a mental health program manager would change service placement or workforce training based on this gap analysis. Treatment gaps reflect supply, demand, and stigma — the output must acknowledge all three.
