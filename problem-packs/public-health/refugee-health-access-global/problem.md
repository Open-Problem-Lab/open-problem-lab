# Refugee Health Service Access Gaps In Host Countries

## Problem Statement

Build a verified workflow for measuring refugee health service access gaps relative to host populations, linking UNHCR health data, national health-system data, and refugee-population data.

> Which evidence-backed signals are sufficient to support intervention targeting without overstating certainty?

## Known Facts

- Verified fact: UNHCR reports that over 110 million people are forcibly displaced globally, with refugee health-service access systematically lower than host populations and dependent on humanitarian funding cycles.

## Uncertain Areas

Whether available data is sufficient for sub-national analysis. Whether gap maps change actual policy or investment decisions. How measurement methodology compares across countries.

## Initial Scope

Evidence inventory, dataset compilation, sub-national gap analysis, red-team review, field-reality review.

## Done Condition For V0

One reviewed and replicated sub-national analysis with explicit uncertainty documentation.
