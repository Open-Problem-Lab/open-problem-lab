# Evidence Ledger

## Evidence Notes

### UNICEF/WHO/World Bank JME 2023

Use this source for global and regional stunting burden framing. The 149 million figure is authoritative. Modeled estimates smooth sub-national variation.

### Bhutta/Lancet 2021

Use this source for evidence-based intervention packages and potential impact. The 20 percent stunting reduction is a modeled potential, not an observed outcome.

### Osgood-Zimmerman et al. 2018 Nature

Use this source for the best available sub-national stunting mapping. The 5km resolution enables hotspot identification but is interpolated from survey data.

## Evidence Quality Rule

Evidence is not accepted because it sounds plausible. It is accepted when the source, method, limitations, and confidence are explicit enough for a reviewer to attack.
