## Evidence Notes

### UNICEF/WHO/World Bank JME 2023

45 million wasted, 13.6 million SAM. Treatment coverage below 30% region-wide.

### Global Nutrition Report 2021

Only 6 of 51 SSA countries on track for wasting reduction. Conflict and climate shocks as barriers.

### Rogers et al. 2022

CMAM achieves >90% recovery at >70% coverage. Median SSA coverage: 27%.

## Evidence Quality Rule

Evidence accepted only when source, method, limitations, and confidence are explicit.
