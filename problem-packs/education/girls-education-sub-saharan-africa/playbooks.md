# Playbooks

## Analyst Playbook

Start with `problem.json`. Add evidence before claims. Mark operational inferences reviewed/replicated/blocked.

## Reviewer Playbook

Ask: What education-gap claim? What survey data? What method? What would make the gap estimate wrong? Who could misuse a gap map to stigmatize communities or justify harmful policies?

## Field-Reality Playbook

Ask whether an education ministry official would change school-fee, infrastructure, or safety programming based on this map. Education gaps have cultural, economic, and safety dimensions that maps alone cannot capture.
