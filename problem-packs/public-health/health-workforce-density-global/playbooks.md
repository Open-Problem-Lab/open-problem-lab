# Playbooks

Start with `problem.json`. Add evidence before claims. Mark inferences reviewed/replicated/blocked.

## Reviewer Playbook

Ask: What workforce gap claim? What registry data? What method? What makes the gap wrong? Who could misuse it to justify forced rural deployment?

## Field-Reality Playbook

Ask whether a district health officer would change recruitment based on this analysis. Workforce distribution reflects deployment policy, working conditions, and career pathways — not just density.
