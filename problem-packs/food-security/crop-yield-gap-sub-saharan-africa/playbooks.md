# Playbooks

## Analyst Playbook

Start with `problem.json`. Add evidence before claims. Mark operational inferences reviewed/replicated/blocked.

## Reviewer Playbook

Ask: What yield gap claim? What satellite or survey data? What estimation method? What makes the gap wrong? Who could misuse a yield gap map to justify land grabs?

## Field-Reality Playbook

Ask whether an extension officer would change input recommendations based on this analysis. Yield gaps reflect soil, water, market access, and farmer knowledge — the output must acknowledge all.
