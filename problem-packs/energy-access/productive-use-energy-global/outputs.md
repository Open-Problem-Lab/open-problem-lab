## Candidate Output Types

- Evidence inventory with mini-grid operator data granularity and agricultural value-chain specificity documentation.
- Sub-national productive-use gap map for at least two LMIC contexts with value-chain specificity.
- Income-impact analysis linking electrification to agricultural productivity with context-dependency documentation.
- Failure-mode review of productive-use overstatement and projected-income-claim risks.
- Field-reality review with mini-grid operator and agricultural extension context.

## Publication Rule

Published outputs must say what they are not. A productive-use gap map is not an income-gain guarantee — electricity access does not equal verified productive use without equipment adoption, affordability, technical capacity, and market access. Projected income gains from electrification must be distinguished from observed productive-use outcomes.
