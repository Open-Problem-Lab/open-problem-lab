# Playbooks

## Analyst Playbook

1. Start with `problem.json`, `evidence.json`, and `tasks.json`.
2. Select one scoped task. Add evidence before claims. Keep outputs reproducible.
3. Mark every program-relevant inference as reviewed, replicated, or blocked.

## Reviewer Playbook

Ask: What stunting claim? What survey data? What method produced it? What would make it wrong? Who could misuse a hotspot map to stigmatize communities?

## Field-Reality Playbook

Ask whether a nutrition program manager would change intervention targeting based on this output, and what would go wrong if the hotspot map was wrong. Nutrition hotspots require context beyond anthropometric data — food access, health system reach, and cultural practices must be acknowledged.
