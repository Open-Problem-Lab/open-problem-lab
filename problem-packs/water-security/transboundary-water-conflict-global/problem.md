# Transboundary Water Conflict Risk In Climate-Stressed River Basins

## Problem Statement

Build a verified workflow for identifying where climate-driven water stress increases transboundary conflict risk, linking water-availability projections, upstream-downstream dependency data, and institutional-capacity data.

> Which evidence-backed signals are sufficient to support decision-making without overstating certainty?

## Known Facts

- Verified fact: World Bank estimates 60 percent of global freshwater flows cross national boundaries, with 150+ transboundary agreements covering fewer than half of the world's shared basins.

## Uncertain Areas

Whether available data is sufficient for sub-national analysis. Whether gap maps change actual policy or investment decisions. How measurement methodology compares across countries.

## Initial Scope

Evidence inventory, dataset compilation, sub-national gap analysis, red-team review, field-reality review.

## Done Condition For V0

One reviewed and replicated sub-national analysis with explicit uncertainty documentation.
