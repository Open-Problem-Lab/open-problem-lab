# Dataset Inventory

## Candidate Sources

| Source                   | Grain                              | Current status              | Use                                         |
| ------------------------ | ---------------------------------- | --------------------------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| WHO dengue sources       | Country/global                     | Usable for framing          | Burden, prevention, and risk-factor context |
| WHO Viet Nam dengue page | Country/subnational summary        | Usable with date caveat     | Seasonality and regional burden framing     |
| Gibb et al. 2023 study   | District-level historical analysis | Usable as research evidence | Candidate variables and replication target  |

## Required Dataset Properties

- Date range.
- Geographic grain.
- Case definition.
- Reporting lag.
- Missingness.
- License or reuse permission.
- Denominator source.
- Known changes in administrative boundaries.

## Rejection Rule

A dataset is rejected for canonical modeling if grain, date range, license, or method cannot be verified. Rejected datasets may still be listed as context.
