# Dataset Inventory

| Source                     | Grain              | Status                    | Use                        |
| -------------------------- | ------------------ | ------------------------- | -------------------------- |
| World Mental Health Survey | Country, irregular | Usable, sparse            | Prevalence estimates       |
| WHO Mental Health Atlas    | Country            | Usable                    | Workforce and service data |
| DHS mental health modules  | Cluster            | Usable, limited countries | Sub-national prevalence    |
| Health facility surveys    | Facility           | Variable                  | Treatment capacity         |
| GBD mental disorder burden | 0.1 deg            | Usable                    | Modeled prevalence         |

## Required Properties

Survey methodology, diagnostic instrument, treatment definition, license.

## Rejection Rule

Rejected if methodology or diagnostic validity unverifiable.
