## Candidate Output Types

- Compliance-verification system coverage assessment across LMIC countries.

- Field-reality review with clean-cooking program and manufacturer context.

## Publication Rule

Published outputs must say what they are not. A laboratory performance tier is not a field-performance guarantee — 30-70 percent degradation is documented. Health-impact claims based on laboratory data without field verification are incomplete. Compliance claims without independent verification coverage data may overstate stove program effectiveness.
