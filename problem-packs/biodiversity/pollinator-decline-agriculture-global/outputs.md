## Candidate Output Types

- Evidence inventory with pollinator-monitoring data gap assessment and LMIC coverage documentation.
- Sub-national crop-pollination dependence map from MapSPAM with classification source documentation.
- Habitat-change analysis linking satellite land-cover trends to pollinator habitat pressure indicators.
- Pollination-service risk map combining crop-pollination dependence and habitat-change proxies.
- Failure-mode review of proxy-based pollinator-decline overstatement.
- Field-reality review with agricultural research and extension context.

## Publication Rule

Published outputs must say what they are not. A pollination-service risk map is not a verified pollinator-decline measurement, a crop-loss forecast, or a basis for pesticide-ban recommendations without direct pollinator monitoring evidence. Proxy-based risk indicators must state explicitly that they are proxies, not observations.
