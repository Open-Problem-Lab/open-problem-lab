## Candidate Output Types

- Evidence inventory with census migration-data comparability assessment and satellite environmental indicator documentation.
- Harmonized district-level migration-flow dataset for at least two South Asian countries.
- Environmental-migration correlation analysis with economic confound controls and variance-explained documentation.
- Failure-mode review of climate-migration attribution overstatement and mobility-restriction risks.
- Field-reality review with urban-planning and adaptation-policy context.

## Publication Rule

Published outputs must say what they are not. An environmental-migration correlation is not proof of climate causation — economic drivers explain more migration variance than environmental signals. Migration maps must not be used to restrict mobility — migration is often the most effective adaptation strategy available to households. Modest correlations (5-15 percent variance explained) must be presented honestly, not as dominant climate-driven displacement.
