# Evidence Ledger

## Evidence Notes

### IPCC AR6 Heat Extremes

South Asia as global hotspot for wet-bulb temperature extremes approaching survivability limits.

### Mazdiyasni et al. 2017

The 2015 heatwave mortality analysis — 2,500 dead, concentrated among outdoor workers and elderly in urban areas.

### Hess/Ahmedabad HAP 2018

Proof of concept that urban heat adaptation plans work. Ahmedabad's estimated 1,000+ deaths averted annually.

## Evidence Quality Rule

Evidence accepted only when source, method, limitations, and confidence are explicit enough for reviewer attack.
