# Early Childhood Development Measurement And Intervention Coverage Gaps

## Problem Statement

Build a verified workflow for measuring early childhood development outcomes and intervention coverage gaps, linking MICS ECDI data, preschool-access data, and nutrition data.

> Which evidence-backed signals are sufficient to support intervention targeting without overstating certainty?

## Known Facts

- Verified fact: WHO/UNICEF Nurturing Care Framework estimates 250 million children under five in LMICs are at risk of not reaching their developmental potential due to poverty, malnutrition, and lack of stimulation.

## Uncertain Areas

Whether available data is sufficient for sub-national analysis. Whether gap maps change actual policy or investment decisions. How measurement methodology compares across countries.

## Initial Scope

Evidence inventory, dataset compilation, sub-national gap analysis, red-team review, field-reality review.

## Done Condition For V0

One reviewed and replicated sub-national analysis with explicit uncertainty documentation.
