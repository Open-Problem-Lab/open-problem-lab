# Evidence Ledger

## Evidence Notes

### ICIMOD HKH Assessment 2019

Landmark regional assessment. The 36-64 percent glacier loss projection is the consensus baseline. Model spread is substantial and must be carried through.

### Pritchard et al. 2019 Nature

Strongest satellite-based evidence for Himalayan mass-loss acceleration. Doubling of loss rates between eras is the key finding.

### Imran et al. 2022

Downstream demand growth context. The compound risk of upstream supply decline and downstream demand growth is the pack's analytical core.

## Evidence Quality Rule

Evidence is not accepted because it sounds plausible — it is accepted when source, method, limitations, and confidence are explicit.
