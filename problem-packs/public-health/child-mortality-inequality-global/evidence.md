# Evidence Ledger

## Evidence Notes

### UNICEF Child Mortality 2024

U5MR fell from 93 to 37 per 1000 since 1990. Poorest-richest ratio exceeds 2:1 in 30+ LMICs.

### Victora/Lancet 2020

Sub-national inequality widened in 40% of LMICs while national averages improved. Poorest districts slowest improvement.

## Evidence Quality Rule

Evidence accepted only when source, method, limitations, and confidence are explicit enough for a reviewer to attack.
