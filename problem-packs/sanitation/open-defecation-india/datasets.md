# Dataset Inventory

## Candidate Sources

| Source                      | Grain               | Current status      | Use                                         |
| --------------------------- | ------------------- | ------------------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| NFHS-5 household survey     | District, 2019-2021 | Usable              | Sanitation access, use, and health outcomes |
| SBM administrative data     | District, annual    | Usable with caveats | Toilet construction claims                  |
| Census 2011 sanitation data | District, 2011      | Usable as baseline  | Pre-SBM sanitation baseline                 |
| JMP country estimates       | Country, annual     | Usable for framing  | International comparison                    |
| DLHS-4 health survey        | District, 2012-2013 | Usable as baseline  | Pre-SBM health baseline                     |

## Required Dataset Properties

- Date range and survey year.
- Geographic grain.
- Sanitation facility type classification.
- Toilet usage versus access distinction.
- Child health outcome data where available.
- License or reuse permission.

## Rejection Rule

A dataset is rejected for canonical modeling if source, date, methodology, or geographic grain cannot be verified.
