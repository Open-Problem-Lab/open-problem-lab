# Evidence Ledger

## Current Evidence Records

The machine-readable ledger is `evidence.json`.

## Evidence Notes

### WHO Household Air Pollution Fact Sheet

Use this source for the global disease burden and health effects of household air pollution. It establishes the scale of the problem but not spatial variation within Sub-Saharan Africa.

### IEA Africa Energy Outlook 2022

Use this source for clean cooking access statistics, investment projections, and technology pathway analysis. The 900-million figure establishes urgency. Projections are modeled, not observed.

### Shupler et al. 2021 Lancet Planetary Health

Use this source for the strongest quantitative estimate of health benefits from clean cooking transition. Treat the 400,000 deaths-averted figure as a directional estimate, not a precise prediction — it assumes idealized adoption.

## Evidence Quality Rule

Evidence is not accepted because it sounds plausible. It is accepted when the source, method, limitations, and confidence are explicit enough for a reviewer to attack.
