# Dataset Inventory

## Candidate Sources

| Source                                | Grain                          | Current status                           | Use                                                      |
| ------------------------------------- | ------------------------------ | ---------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------- |
| TASAI — African Seed Access Index     | Country, 20+ African countries | Usable, publicly available               | Seed-sector infrastructure and policy environment        |
| CGIAR variety-release catalogs        | Country, by crop               | Usable, publicly available               | Climate-resilient variety availability by crop and country |
| LSMS-ISA agricultural surveys         | Household, sub-national        | Usable, publicly available               | Farmer adoption rates and seed-source data               |
| DHS agricultural modules              | Household, sub-national        | Usable, publicly available               | Seed access and crop-variety use indicators              |
| On-farm trial databases (CGIAR/NAVS)  | Site-point, crop-specific      | Usable, limited coverage                 | On-farm yield performance under farmer conditions        |
| National seed-certification records   | Country, annual                | Variable by country                      | Certified seed production and distribution volumes       |
| Farmer-preference studies             | Site-point, crop-specific      | Sparse, publication-dependent            | Trait-preference and adoption-decision context           |
| Seed-company distribution data        | Company, variable              | Limited, often proprietary               | Private-sector seed distribution and market reach        |
| Agro-ecological zone maps             | Sub-national, static           | Usable, publicly available               | Climate-context for variety-environment matching         |

## Required Dataset Properties

- Date range and temporal resolution.
- Geographic grain and spatial resolution.
- Crop-country coverage assessment.
- Access conditions and license.
- Adoption-measurement methodology — explicitly document how adoption is measured (farmer survey, seed sales, variety-use observation).
- On-farm vs. on-station yield data availability.

## Rejection Rule

A dataset is rejected for canonical analysis if grain, date range, license, or method cannot be verified. Datasets with known gaps (e.g., TASAI Africa-only, proprietary seed-company data) may be listed as limited with explicit documentation. Rejected datasets may still be listed as context.
