# Playbooks

## Analyst Playbook

Start with `problem.json`. Add evidence before claims. Mark operational inferences reviewed/replicated/blocked.

## Reviewer Playbook

Ask: What exposure claim? What fuel and ventilation data? What method? What makes the exposure estimate dangerously wrong? Could targeting based on primary fuel alone miss the most exposed?

## Field-Reality Playbook

Ask whether a clean-cooking program manager would change technology or behavior-change targeting based on this exposure map. Fuel stacking means that primary-fuel surveys alone are insufficient — every output must acknowledge this.
