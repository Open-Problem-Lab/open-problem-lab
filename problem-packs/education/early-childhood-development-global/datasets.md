## Required Dataset Properties

- Date range and temporal resolution.
- Geographic grain and spatial resolution.
- ECD-measurement instrument comparability — explicitly document across countries and survey rounds.
- Sub-national sample sufficiency for district-level analysis.
- Intervention-coverage data granularity.
- Access conditions and license.

## Rejection Rule

A dataset is rejected for canonical analysis if grain, date range, license, or method cannot be verified. Datasets with known limitations (e.g., ECDI instrument variation, limited country coverage) may be listed as limited with explicit documentation. Rejected datasets may still be listed as context.
