# Playbooks

## Analyst Playbook

1. Start with `problem.json`, `evidence.json`, and `tasks.json`. 2. Add evidence before claims. 3. Keep outputs reproducible. 4. Mark operational inferences as reviewed, replicated, or blocked.

## Reviewer Playbook

Ask: What melt-risk claim? What projection data? What method? What would make it wrong? Who could misuse a water-scarcity projection to justify upstream dam construction?

## Field-Reality Playbook

Ask whether a basin water manager would change allocation or infrastructure planning based on this output, and what would happen if the melt projection was wrong. Transboundary water politics make every risk claim potentially weaponizable.
