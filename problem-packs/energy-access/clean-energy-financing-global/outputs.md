## Candidate Output Types

- Evidence inventory with investment-flow data granularity and financing-structure barrier documentation.
- Sub-national clean energy financing gap map for at least two low-income countries.
- Financing-structure analysis documenting missing-middle barriers, concessional-finance allocation, and de-risking instrument coverage.
- Failure-mode review of financing-gap overstatement and volume-implies-access risks.
- Field-reality review with development-finance institution and energy-enterprise context.

## Publication Rule

Published outputs must say what they are not. A financing-gap map is not an investment recommendation — aggregate investment statistics mask sub-national targeting needs. Increasing finance volume alone does not close access gaps — financing structure, instruments, and intermediation all matter. Missing-middle barriers require structural solutions, not just more capital.
