# Playbooks

## Analyst Playbook

Start with `problem.json`. Add evidence before claims. Mark operational inferences reviewed/replicated/blocked.

## Reviewer Playbook

Ask: What disease-risk claim? What surveillance and satellite data? What method? What makes a risk signal a false alarm? Who could misuse a disease-risk map to justify herd culling or market closures that harm pastoralists?

## Field-Reality Playbook

Ask whether a veterinary services director would change vaccination campaigns or market restrictions based on this analysis. Disease-surveillance data in pastoralist areas has severe underreporting bias.
