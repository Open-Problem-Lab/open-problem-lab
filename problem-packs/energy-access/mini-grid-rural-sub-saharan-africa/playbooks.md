# Playbooks

## Analyst Playbook

Start with `problem.json`. Add evidence before claims. Mark operational inferences reviewed/replicated/blocked.

## Reviewer Playbook

Ask: What viability claim? What spatial data? What method? What would make viability classification wrong? Who could misuse a viability map to justify bad investment?

## Field-Reality Playbook

Ask whether a rural electrification agency would change their mini-grid site selection based on this map. Viability is not just solar resource — it is community demand, willingness to pay, and maintenance capacity.
