# Playbooks

## Analyst Playbook

Start with `problem.json`. Add evidence before claims. Mark operational inferences reviewed/replicated/blocked.

## Reviewer Playbook

Ask: What migration-environment claim? What census and satellite data? What method? What confounds the correlation? Who could misuse migration maps to justify restricting mobility?

## Field-Reality Playbook

Ask whether a district administrator or urban planner would change service provision based on this analysis. Migration is not a problem to be stopped — it is often the most effective adaptation strategy available to households.
