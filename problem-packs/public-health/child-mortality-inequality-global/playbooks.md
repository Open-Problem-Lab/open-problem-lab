# Playbooks

## Analyst Playbook

Start with `problem.json`, `evidence.json`, and `tasks.json`. Add evidence before claims. Keep quantitative outputs reproducible. Mark every policy-relevant inference as reviewed, replicated, or blocked.

## Reviewer Playbook

Ask: What inequality claim? What DHS mortality and wealth data? What method? What would make the inequality trend wrong? Who could misuse an inequality map to penalize districts that are poor and data-sparse?

## Field-Reality Playbook

Ask whether a district health officer or ministry equity unit would change resource allocation or outreach strategy based on this output. Inequality maps can stigmatize poor-performing districts — the output must acknowledge that district-level mortality estimates have wide uncertainty in small populations.
