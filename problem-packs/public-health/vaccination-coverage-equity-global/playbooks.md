# Playbooks

Start with `problem.json`. Add evidence before claims. Mark inferences reviewed/replicated/blocked.

## Reviewer Playbook

Ask: What equity-gap claim? What survey data? What method? What makes the gap wrong? Who could misuse an equity map to penalize districts?

## Field-Reality Playbook

Ask whether an EPI manager would change outreach based on this analysis. Zero-dose children are missed for reasons beyond geography — demand, trust, and gender barriers matter.
