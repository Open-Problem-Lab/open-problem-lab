## Candidate Output Types

- Evidence inventory with MICS ECDI instrument-comparability assessment and sub-national data availability documentation.
- Sub-national ECD-outcome map for at least two LMICs using MICS ECDI data.
- Intervention-coverage analysis linking ECD outcomes to preschool access, parenting programs, and nutrition interventions.
- Failure-mode review of ECD-measurement overstatement and coverage-implies-impact risks.
- Field-reality review with ECD ministry and program-implementer context.

## Publication Rule

Published outputs must say what they are not. An ECD-outcome map is not an intervention-effectiveness measurement — coverage does not equal verified developmental impact. MICS ECDI instrument variation limits cross-country comparison. Parenting-intervention trial results may not replicate at program scale.
