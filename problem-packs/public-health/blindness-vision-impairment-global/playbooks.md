# Playbooks

Start with `problem.json`. Add evidence before claims. Mark inferences reviewed/replicated/blocked.

## Reviewer Playbook

Ask: What CSC gap claim? What RAAB data? What method? Who could misuse a gap map to justify cataract outreach without quality standards?

## Field-Reality Playbook

Ask whether an eye-health program manager would change surgical-camp placement based on this. CSC gaps reflect workforce, facility, and demand barriers — not just surgical-volume deficits.
