# Outputs

## Accepted Outputs

No accepted outputs exist yet.

This is deliberate. The pack is scoped, not solved.

## Candidate Output Types

- Evidence and data source inventory.
- Aquifer-level groundwater storage anomaly time series.
- District-level depletion trend analysis with uncertainty bounds.
- Agricultural demand correlation with groundwater decline.
- Failure-mode review.
- Field-reality review.

## Publication Rule

Published outputs must say what they are not. A depletion trend map is not a water rationing order, cropping mandate, or electricity pricing policy recommendation.
