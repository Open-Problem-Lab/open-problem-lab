# Urban Food Desert Mapping And Fresh-Food Access Gaps In LMIC Cities

## Problem Statement

Build a verified workflow for mapping urban food deserts and fresh-food access gaps in LMIC cities, linking market-location data, food-price data, and population data.

> Which evidence-backed signals are sufficient to support intervention targeting without overstating certainty?

## Known Facts

- Verified fact: FAO reports that 2.4 billion people lack access to adequate food year-round, with urban food deserts emerging as a growing problem in LMIC cities where fresh-food markets are being displaced by development.

## Uncertain Areas

Whether available data is sufficient for sub-national analysis. Whether gap maps change actual policy or investment decisions. How measurement methodology compares across countries.

## Initial Scope

Evidence inventory, dataset compilation, sub-national gap analysis, red-team review, field-reality review.

## Done Condition For V0

One reviewed and replicated sub-national analysis with explicit uncertainty documentation.
