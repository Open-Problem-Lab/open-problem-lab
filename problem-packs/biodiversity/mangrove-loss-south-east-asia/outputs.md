## Candidate Output Types

- Evidence inventory with GMW product comparison and storm-surge model assessment.
- Sub-national mangrove deforestation map with driver classification for at least two Southeast Asian countries.
- Coastal-protection loss analysis linking deforestation to reduced storm-surge attenuation.
- Remaining-mangrove threat assessment identifying highest-pressure conversion zones.
- Failure-mode review of protection-valuation overstatement and displacement risks.
- Field-reality review with coastal-zone management and restoration context.

## Publication Rule

Published outputs must say what they are not. A protection-loss valuation is not an aquaculture-ban justification, a coastal-development moratorium basis, or a mangrove-carbon-credit claim. Deforestation-driver attribution is not individual-farmer culpability documentation.
