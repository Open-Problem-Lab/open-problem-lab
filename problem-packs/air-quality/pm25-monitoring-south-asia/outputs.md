## Candidate Output Types

- Evidence and data source inventory.
- Ground monitoring network gap map for India and at least one other South Asian country.
- Monitoring gap priority ranking by population, baseline concentration, and uncertainty.
- Low-cost sensor validation against reference monitors where co-located data exists.
- Failure-mode review.
- Field-reality review with pollution control board context.

## Publication Rule

Published outputs must say what they are not. A monitoring gap map is not a sensor deployment order, a regulatory standard recommendation, or a health impact assessment.
