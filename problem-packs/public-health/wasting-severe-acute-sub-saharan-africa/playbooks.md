# Playbooks

## Analyst Playbook

Start with `problem.json`. Add evidence before claims. Mark operational inferences reviewed/replicated/blocked.

## Reviewer Playbook

Ask: What treatment coverage gap claim? What prevalence and program data? What reconciliation method? What would make the gap estimate dangerously wrong? Who could misuse it to divert RUTF from districts with higher burden but lower reporting?

## Field-Reality Playbook

Ask whether a district nutrition coordinator would change RUTF distribution or outreach based on this gap analysis. Treatment coverage gaps have supply-chain, demand-side, and health-system dimensions — the output must acknowledge all three.
