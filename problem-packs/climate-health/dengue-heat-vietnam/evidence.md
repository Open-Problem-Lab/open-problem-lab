## Evidence Notes

### WHO Dengue Fact Sheet

Use this source for global dengue burden, transmission, prevention, and high-level risk factors. Do not use it as proof of a Viet Nam district threshold.

### WHO Viet Nam Dengue Page

Use this source for country-level seasonality and regional burden framing. Its fetched page did not expose a clear update date, so claims from this source should be rechecked before public release.

### Gibb et al. 2023

Use this source for the strongest initial evidence that Viet Nam dengue risk is shaped by interacting climate, infrastructure, and mobility factors. Treat it as a hypothesis generator for replication, not as an automatic alert engine.

## Evidence Quality Rule

Evidence is not accepted because it sounds plausible. It is accepted when the source, method, limitations, and confidence are explicit enough for a reviewer to attack.
