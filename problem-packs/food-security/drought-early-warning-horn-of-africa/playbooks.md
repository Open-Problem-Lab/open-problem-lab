# Playbooks

## Analyst Playbook

Start with `problem.json`. Add evidence before claims. Mark operational inferences as reviewed/replicated/blocked.

## Reviewer Playbook

Ask: What food-insecurity prediction? What signal data? What method? What makes it a false alarm? Who could misuse a forecast to divert aid from invisible crises?

## Field-Reality Playbook

Ask whether a humanitarian coordinator would preposition supplies based on this signal. False alarms waste scarce aid resources and erode donor trust — false negatives cost lives.
