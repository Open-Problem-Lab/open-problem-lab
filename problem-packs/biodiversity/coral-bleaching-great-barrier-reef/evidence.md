# Evidence Ledger

## Evidence Notes

### GBRMPA Reef Health

Authoritative source for GBR bleaching event timeline and severity.

### NOAA Coral Reef Watch

Satellite-based bleaching alert system. DHW-SST temperature gap of 1-3°C is critical limitation.

### Eakin et al. 2022

Satellite metrics explain ~70% of bleaching variance. Local factors account for the rest.

## Evidence Quality Rule

Evidence accepted only when source, method, limitations, and confidence are explicit.
