| IRENA investment statistics           | Country, annual                | Usable, publicly available               | Clean energy investment flows by country and technology  |

## Required Dataset Properties

- Date range and temporal resolution.
- Geographic grain and spatial resolution.
- Sub-national data availability — explicitly document where financing data is national aggregate only.
- Financing-structure documentation — document which barriers (missing middle, currency risk, transaction costs) are captured.
- Concessional-finance allocation data — document country-income-group and technology-type breakdowns.
- Access conditions and license.

## Rejection Rule

A dataset is rejected for canonical analysis if grain, date range, license, or method cannot be verified. Datasets with known limitations (e.g., national-only aggregates, subscription-only access) may be listed as limited with explicit documentation. Rejected datasets may still be listed as context.
