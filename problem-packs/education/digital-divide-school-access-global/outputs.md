## Publication Rule

Published outputs must say what they are not. A connectivity map is not a digital-readiness assessment — connectivity without electricity, devices, teacher digital literacy, and relevant content does not enable digital learning. Satellite-derived connectivity classifications may misclassify intermittent or mobile-only connectivity. Connectivity maps must not drive technology procurement without complementary investments.
