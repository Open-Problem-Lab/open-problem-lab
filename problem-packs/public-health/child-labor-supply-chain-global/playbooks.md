# Playbooks

## Analyst Playbook

Start with `problem.json`. Add evidence before claims. Mark operational inferences reviewed/replicated/blocked.

## Reviewer Playbook

Ask: What child-labor risk claim? What survey and crop data? What method? What makes the risk map wrong? Who could misuse it to justify trade bans that harm smallholder livelihoods?

## Field-Reality Playbook

Ask whether a supply-chain due-diligence officer would change sourcing or remediation based on this risk map. Child labor in agriculture often reflects household poverty — risk maps must not penalize the poorest farmers.
