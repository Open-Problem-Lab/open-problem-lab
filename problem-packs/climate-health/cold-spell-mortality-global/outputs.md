## Candidate Output Types

- Evidence inventory with temperature-mortality extrapolation assessment and LMIC data-gap documentation.
- Sub-national cold-spell frequency and intensity map for at least two LMIC high-altitude or cold-exposed regions.
- Heating-access vulnerability analysis combining cold-spell exposure with DHS housing-quality proxies.
- Failure-mode review of cold-mortality extrapolation overstatement and relative-priority risks.
- Field-reality review with housing, energy, and health authority context.

## Publication Rule

Published outputs must say what they are not. A cold-mortality risk map is not a verified mortality estimate — it is a proxy-based risk indicator extrapolated from high-income country studies with unvalidated housing-quality proxies. Cold-risk analysis must not be used to deprioritize heat-adaptation investments without explicit dual-hazard context.
