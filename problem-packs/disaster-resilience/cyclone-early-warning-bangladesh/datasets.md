## Required Dataset Properties

- Date range and temporal resolution.
- Geographic grain and coordinate reference system.
- Storm identifier and observation time.
- Intensity metric and units (note averaging period: 1-min, 3-min, or 10-min).
- Population estimate year and methodology.
- Shelter capacity and condition information.
- Bathymetry version and source data type (multibeam, altimetry, single-beam).

## Rejection Rule

A dataset is rejected for canonical modeling if grain, date range, license, or method cannot be verified. Rejected datasets may still be listed as context. Datasets classified as limited are usable for specific purposes but cannot serve as sole evidence for quantitative claims.
