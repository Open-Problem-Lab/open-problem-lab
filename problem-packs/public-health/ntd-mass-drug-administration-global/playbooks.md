# Playbooks

Start with `problem.json`. Add evidence before claims. Mark inferences reviewed/replicated/blocked.

## Reviewer Playbook

Ask: What coverage-gap claim? What PCT and survey data? What validation method? Who could misuse it to cut MDA funding to low-reported districts?

## Field-Reality Playbook

Ask whether an NTD program manager would change MDA targeting based on this. Low coverage may reflect reporting failure, supply-chain issues, or community refusal — maps must acknowledge all.
