# Playbooks

## Analyst Playbook

Start with `problem.json`. Add evidence before claims. Mark operational inferences reviewed/replicated/blocked.

## Reviewer Playbook

Ask: What service-disruption claim? What conflict and facility data? What method? What makes the disruption estimate wrong? Who could misuse a disruption map to justify withdrawal of health services from conflict-affected areas?

## Field-Reality Playbook

Ask whether a humanitarian health cluster coordinator would change mobile-clinic deployment based on this analysis. Disruption maps in active conflict carry security risks for health workers.
