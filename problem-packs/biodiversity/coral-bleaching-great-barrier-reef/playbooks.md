# Playbooks

## Analyst Playbook

Start with `problem.json`. Add evidence before claims. Mark operational inferences as reviewed/replicated/blocked.

## Reviewer Playbook

Ask: What bleaching claim? What satellite and survey data? What method? What would make severity classification wrong? Who could misuse a bleaching map to justify reef abandonment?

## Field-Reality Playbook

Ask whether a reef manager would change protection zoning or restoration investment based on this output. Bleaching maps that show uniform mortality where patchy survival exists can discourage conservation effort.
