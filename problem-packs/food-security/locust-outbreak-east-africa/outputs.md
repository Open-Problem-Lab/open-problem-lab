## Publication Rule

Published outputs must say what they are not. A locust outbreak risk map is not a control operation order, pesticide recommendation, or crop-loss forecast. A breeding habitat suitability baseline is not a swarm forecast. A trajectory hindcast is not a real-time prediction.
