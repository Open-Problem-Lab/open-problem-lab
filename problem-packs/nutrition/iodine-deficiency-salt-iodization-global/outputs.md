# Outputs

## Accepted Outputs

No accepted outputs exist yet.

This is deliberate. The pack is scoped, not solved.

## Candidate Output Types

- Evidence inventory.
- Dataset inventory.
- Sub-national iodine status and salt iodization coverage map for one to three LMICs.
- Coverage gap analysis with population at risk estimates.
- Salt iodization program intervention evidence review.
- Failure-mode review.
- Field-reality review.

## Publication Rule

Published outputs must say what they are not. A coverage gap map is not a salt iodization enforcement directive, a producer compliance determination, or a clinical iodine supplementation guide. It does not assign blame to salt producers, governments, or consumers for coverage gaps.
