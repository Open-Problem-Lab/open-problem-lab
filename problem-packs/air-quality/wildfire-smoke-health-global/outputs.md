# Outputs

## Accepted Outputs

No accepted outputs exist yet.

This is deliberate. The first pack is scoped, not solved.

## Candidate Output Types

- Evidence inventory with satellite smoke-detection product comparison and LMIC ground-monitor coverage assessment.
- Smoke-exposure validation report documenting satellite-to-monitor correlation during smoke and non-smoke periods across different fire regimes.
- Sub-national smoke-specific PM2.5 exposure map with documented GEOS-Chem composition methodology and HMS plume integration.
- Respiratory health-impact analysis with confound controls for non-smoke PM2.5, temperature, and respiratory seasonality.
- Failure-mode review of exposure misclassification, health-impact overstatement, and panic-based misuse.
- Field-reality review with air-quality management and health-ministry context.

## Publication Rule

Published outputs must say what they are not. A smoke-health impact estimate is not an air-quality advisory, an evacuation recommendation, or a basis for banning prescribed burning. Smoke-specific PM2.5 estimates are not ground-truth measurements without monitor validation.
