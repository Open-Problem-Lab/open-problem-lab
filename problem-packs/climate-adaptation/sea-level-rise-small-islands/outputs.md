# Outputs

## Accepted Outputs

No accepted outputs exist yet.

## Candidate Output Types

- Evidence and data source inventory.
- Island-scale coastal exposure maps under multiple SLR scenarios.
- Compound-risk analysis combining SLR, storm surge, and coastal erosion.
- Adaptation prioritization framework with uncertainty communication.
- Failure-mode review including maladaptation risk.
- Field-reality review with SIDS planning context.

## Publication Rule

Published outputs must say what they are not. A coastal exposure map is not a relocation recommendation, an adaptation investment allocation, a migration policy, or a definitive local SLR projection.
