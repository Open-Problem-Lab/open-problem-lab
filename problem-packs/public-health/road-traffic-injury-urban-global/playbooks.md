# Playbooks

## Analyst Playbook

Start with `problem.json`. Add evidence before claims. Mark operational inferences reviewed/replicated/blocked.

## Reviewer Playbook

Ask: What crash-to-trauma access claim? What crash and facility data? What method? What makes the map dangerously wrong? Could crash-data bias toward police-reported crashes misdirect trauma resources?

## Field-Reality Playbook

Ask whether an emergency medical service director would change ambulance station placement or trauma-center designation based on this output. Crash data from police reports undercounts pedestrians and cyclists — the most vulnerable road users.
