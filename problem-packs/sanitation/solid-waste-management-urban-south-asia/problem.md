# Municipal Solid Waste Collection Gaps In South Asian Cities

## Problem Statement

South Asian cities generate over 200 million tons of solid waste annually. Collection rates stay below 60 percent in most municipalities. Uncollected waste is dumped in open lots, waterways, or burned — causing respiratory disease, contaminating groundwater, and emitting methane. Informal waste pickers handle 15-30 percent of recycling but are invisible in official statistics. The question is not whether waste collection is inadequate — it is where the gap is largest at neighborhood scale and what informal systems fill it.

> Which urban neighborhoods in South Asian cities have the highest evidence-backed gap between waste generation and formal collection, and what role do informal recycling systems play?

## Known Facts

- Verified fact: World Bank estimates South Asian cities generate over 200 million tons of solid waste annually, with collection rates below 60 percent in most municipalities and open dumping as the primary disposal method.
- Verified fact: Informal waste pickers handle an estimated 15-30 percent of recycling in South Asian cities, representing 1-2 percent of the urban population and achieving higher recycling rates than formal systems in many neighborhoods.
- Verified fact: Open burning of uncollected waste and landfill fires are major sources of PM2.5 and black carbon in South Asian cities, contributing to the region's severe air pollution.
- Verified fact: Municipal waste-generation data is often extrapolated from national averages rather than neighborhood-level measurement.

## Uncertain Areas

Whether satellite imagery can detect informal dump sites and open burning locations at neighborhood scale. How informal-sector recycling data can be integrated with municipal collection data. Whether gap maps change municipal waste-management investment.

## Initial Scope

Evidence inventory, waste-generation estimation, collection-gap mapping, informal-sector characterization, red-team review, field-reality review.

## Done Condition For V0

One reviewed and replicated neighborhood-scale waste-collection gap analysis for at least two South Asian cities.
