# Playbooks

## Analyst Playbook

Start with `problem.json`. Add evidence before claims. Mark inferences reviewed/replicated/blocked.

## Reviewer Playbook

Ask: What detection-gap claim? What notification and prevalence data? What method? What makes the gap wrong? Who could misuse it to penalize private providers without providing support?

## Field-Reality Playbook

Ask whether a national TB program manager would change active-case-finding deployment based on this analysis. Private providers need support to notify, not just mandates.
