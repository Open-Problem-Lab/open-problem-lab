# Dataset Inventory

| Source | Grain | Status | Use |
| ------ | ----- | ------ | --- |
| OIE WAHIS disease reports | Country, periodic | Usable with gaps | Reported disease outbreaks |
| MODIS NDVI | 250m, 2000-present | Usable | Forage condition monitoring |
| CHIRPS rainfall | 0.05 deg | Usable | Rainfall anomaly for RVF risk |
| Livestock market price data | Market, weekly | Variable | Price anomaly detection |
| Livestock population census | District | Variable, dated | Herd size denominator |

## Required Properties

Disease case definition, reporting date and location, NDVI methodology, market price source, license.

## Rejection Rule

Rejected if disease reporting or forage methodology unverifiable.
