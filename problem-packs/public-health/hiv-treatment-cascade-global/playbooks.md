# Playbooks

## Analyst Playbook

Start with `problem.json`. Add evidence before claims. Mark inferences reviewed/replicated/blocked.

## Reviewer Playbook

Ask: What cascade gap claim? What survey and program data? What reconciliation method? Who could misuse a gap map to cut funding to districts with low reported performance?

## Field-Reality Playbook

Ask whether a district HIV coordinator would change outreach or retention strategies based on this analysis. Low program performance may reflect data-system weakness, not program failure.
