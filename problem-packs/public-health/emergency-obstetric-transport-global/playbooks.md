# Playbooks

## Analyst Playbook

Start with `problem.json`, `evidence.json`, and `tasks.json`. Add evidence before claims. Keep outputs reproducible. Mark operational inferences as reviewed, replicated, or blocked.

## Reviewer Playbook

Ask: What transport-gap claim? What travel-time and ambulance data? What method? What would make the gap estimate dangerously wrong? Who could misuse a transport-gap map to justify closing facilities in remote areas?

## Field-Reality Playbook

Ask whether a district health officer would change ambulance-station placement or community-transport schemes based on this output. Travel-time maps without on-the-ground ambulance data produce false precision — every output must acknowledge the ambulance-data gap.
