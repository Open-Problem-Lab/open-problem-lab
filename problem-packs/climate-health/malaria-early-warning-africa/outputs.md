## Candidate Output Types

- Evidence inventory.
- Dataset inventory with access status and limitations.
- Sub-national malaria baseline notebook or script.
- Climate signal correlation review.
- Back-test report with false-positive and false-negative profiles.
- Failure-mode review.
- Field-reality review.

## Publication Rule

Published outputs must say what they are not. A malaria risk map is not a medical recommendation, outbreak declaration, or resource-allocation order.
