# Evidence Ledger

## Evidence Notes

### Victora/Lancet 2016

Universal breastfeeding could prevent 820,000 child deaths annually — single most effective intervention.

### UNICEF Breastfeeding Scorecard 2024

Global Breastfeeding Scorecard 2023: 46% early initiation within one hour of birth and 48% exclusive breastfeeding under six months. Lowest in West/Central Africa and South Asia. (Corrected June 2026 to the verifiable 2023 edition; prior 44% early-initiation figure updated to 46%.)

## Evidence Quality Rule

Evidence accepted only when source, method, limitations, and confidence are explicit enough for a reviewer to attack.
