# Urban Green Space Cooling Effect And Heat Mortality Reduction Potential

## Problem Statement

Build a verified workflow for measuring urban green space cooling effects and estimating heat-mortality reduction potential in LMIC cities.

> Which evidence-backed signals are sufficient to support decision-making without overstating certainty?

## Known Facts

- Verified fact: A 2022 meta-analysis found urban green spaces reduce ambient temperature by 1-5°C, with the cooling effect extending 100-500 meters, and estimated 2-5 percent heat-mortality reduction per 10 percent green-space increase.

## Uncertain Areas

Whether available data is sufficient for sub-national analysis. Whether gap maps change actual policy or investment decisions. How measurement methodology compares across countries.

## Initial Scope

Evidence inventory, dataset compilation, sub-national gap analysis, red-team review, field-reality review.

## Done Condition For V0

One reviewed and replicated sub-national analysis with explicit uncertainty documentation.
