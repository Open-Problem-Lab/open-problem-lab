## Candidate Output Types

- Evidence inventory with satellite fire-detection product comparison and ground-monitor coverage assessment.
- Fire-PM2.5 validation report documenting satellite-to-ground correlation during burning and non-burning periods.
- Sub-national source-attribution analysis separating agricultural burning PM2.5 from residential, industrial, vehicular, and Diwali-period sources.
- Failure-mode review of source-attribution overstatement and intervention misdirection.
- Field-reality review with pollution control boards and agricultural extension context.

## Publication Rule

Published outputs must say what they are not. A source-attribution map is not an emission inventory, an individual farmer citation, or a basis for punitive enforcement without alternative residue-management options.
