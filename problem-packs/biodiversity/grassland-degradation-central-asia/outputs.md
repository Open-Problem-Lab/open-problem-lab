## Publication Rule

Published outputs must say what they are not. A grassland degradation map is not a rangeland restriction justification, a livestock insurance claim basis, or a pastoral livelihood impact measurement. NDVI trends are productivity indicators — they do not prove irreversible degradation without climate disambiguation, and they do not prove pastoral welfare decline without direct livelihood data.
