## Candidate Output Types

- Evidence inventory with satellite ash-detection product comparison and regional monitoring-network coverage assessment.
- Regional ash-detection capability map for at least two volcanic regions with false-positive and false-negative documentation.
- Ash-concentration forecast uncertainty analysis with eruption-source parameter sensitivity.
- Failure-mode review of detection overstatement and false-negative safety risks.
- Field-reality review with aviation safety authority and airline operations context.

## Publication Rule

Published outputs must say what they are not. A detection-capability map is not a real-time ash alert, an airspace closure recommendation, or a safe-flight threshold determination. Forecast uncertainty estimates are not definitive ash-concentration guidance. Detection gaps in poorly monitored regions must be explicitly documented as aviation safety risks.
