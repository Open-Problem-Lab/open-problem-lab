| --------------- | ----------------------- |

| Reproducibility | Rerunnable              |
| Review          | Approvers               |

## Baseline Requirements

Child labor data must document: survey source, definition, sample methodology, comparability.

## Model Requirements

No supply-chain risk map validated without: attribution methodology, probability caveats, replication.
