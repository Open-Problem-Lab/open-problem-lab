# Playbooks

## Analyst Playbook

Start with `problem.json`. Add evidence before claims. Mark operational inferences reviewed/replicated/blocked.

## Reviewer Playbook

Ask: What flood-risk claim? What impervious and drainage data? What method? What makes the risk map wrong? Who could misuse it to justify eviction in flood-prone informal settlements?

## Field-Reality Playbook

Ask whether a municipal engineer would change drainage-upgrade priorities based on this map. Flood risk in informal settlements is a drainage, land-tenure, and poverty problem — maps alone cannot capture all three.
