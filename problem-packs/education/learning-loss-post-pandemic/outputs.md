## Candidate Output Types

- Evidence and data source inventory.
- Learning loss estimation methodology with uncertainty tiering.
- Country-specific learning loss reconstruction for at least two low-income countries.
- Learning recovery tracking from post-pandemic assessment data.
- Failure-mode review including over-precision and defeatism risks.
- Field-reality review with education ministry context.

## Publication Rule

Published outputs must say what they are not. A learning loss estimate is not a student-level diagnosis, a budget allocation recommendation, or a curriculum mandate.
