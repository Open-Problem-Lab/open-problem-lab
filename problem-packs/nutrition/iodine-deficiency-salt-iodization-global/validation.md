# Validation

## Validation Layers

| Layer           | Gate                                                                 |
| --------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Structure       | `pnpm validate` passes schemas and problem-pack completeness checks  |
| Evidence        | Every claim has a dated evidence record with method and limitations  |
| Reproducibility | Coverage estimates include rerunnable compilation steps              |
| Review          | Required reviewer roles approve or request changes                   |
| Replication     | Safety-sensitive or quantitative claims are independently reproduced |

## Baseline Requirements

Sub-national iodine status estimates must document:

- Survey source and year.
- UIC measurement method and laboratory protocol.
- Population group (school-age children, pregnant women, lactating women).
- WHO UIC cutoff applied.
- Geographic grain (national, regional, provincial, cluster).
- Sample size and sampling design.
- Missingness handling.
- Administrative-boundary changes.

## Coverage Estimate Requirements

No salt iodization coverage estimate may be called actionable until it includes:

- Salt testing method (quantitative titration vs qualitative test kit).
- Adequately iodized threshold definition (ppm range).
- Coverage level measured (household, retail, production).
- Survey year and temporal currency flag.
- Known non-use cases.
- Replication status.

## Intervention Review Requirements

No intervention recommendation may be merged without:

- Named decision-maker.
- Named allocation decision it would change.
- Efficacy evidence with study type and context.
- Cost estimate or range.
- At least one failure mode.
- Non-use cases.
