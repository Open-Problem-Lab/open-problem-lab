# Evidence Ledger

## Evidence Notes

### un-water-wastewater-2023

UN-Water SDG 6.3.1 monitoring estimates about 56 percent of domestic wastewater was safely treated globally in 2022 (no change since 2020); the safely-treated share can be computed for only a minority of countries. (Corrected June 2026: the earlier '80 percent untreated' figure came from an older, differently-scoped source.)

## Evidence Quality Rule

Evidence accepted only when source, method, limitations, and confidence are explicit enough for a reviewer to attack.
