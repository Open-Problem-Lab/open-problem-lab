# Evidence Ledger

## Current Evidence Records

The machine-readable ledger is `evidence.json`.

## Evidence Notes

### WHO Malaria Fact Sheet

Use this source for global malaria burden, transmission patterns, control strategies, and climate context. Do not use it as proof of a district-level threshold.

### WHO World Malaria Report 2024

Use this source for global burden trends and climate-change concern statements. Its climate statements are directional, not quantitative thresholds suitable for operational use.

### Lowbridge et al. 2021 Systematic Review

Use this source for the strongest initial evidence on climate-informed malaria early warning system performance. Treat findings as a map of what has been attempted and what failed, not as a prescription for any specific country's system.

## Evidence Quality Rule

Evidence is not accepted because it sounds plausible. It is accepted when the source, method, limitations, and confidence are explicit enough for a reviewer to attack.
