## Publication Rule

Published outputs must say what they are not. A training-availability map is not an effectiveness measurement — enrollment does not equal employment impact. Pooled effect estimates mask enormous program-design heterogeneity. Informal-sector employment is not captured in formal outcome data. Training-gap maps must not be used for program expansion without outcome evidence.
